Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

The one breath a day technique sounded like a good one for me. I easily manage to fit in at least one breath a day. It's something I can just do when I think of it and not have to try planning a time to do it. I think this way I will be much more relaxed and get more out of it than if I set aside a specific time each day. I imagine most times I will be able to manage much more than one breath and it could start to become a much more natural habit.
