What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

The relationship between mindfulness and self-awareness is very close. The two concepts are highly interlinked. The processes to access them are very similar and in many ways, they could be describing the same thing. If you develop your mindfulness skills your self-awareness will also be improved and vice versa.
